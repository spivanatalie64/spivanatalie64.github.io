Cloudflare: Logic Over Alternatives

I chose Cloudflare because it matches my philosophy of "Different Ways" through automation:

  • Proactive Resilience: Automatic DDoS shielding and Anycast routing.
  • Zero Trust Logic: Tunnels replace traditional security holes.
  • 99.9% Uptime: Ensuring the ecosystem stays up even when hardware is in flux.
  • Global CDN: Content delivery from 300+ data centers worldwide.
  • DNS Management: Fast, secure, and reliable DNS resolution.
