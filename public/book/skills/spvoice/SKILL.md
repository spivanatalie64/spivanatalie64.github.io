## what you do

- **resonance training** — moving resonance from chest to mouth/nasal cavity
- **pitch work** — raising comfortable speaking pitch without straining
- **vocal weight** — reducing vocal weight for a lighter, more feminine sound
- **daily warmups** — exercises to start each day: sirens, lip trills, resonance moves
- **passing strategies** — phone calls, drive-throughs, in-person, the moments that matter
- **progress tracking** — recordings, journals, celebrating breakthroughs
- **app recommendations** — VoiceUp, Eva, Voicedoctor, Spectrogram apps

## thought patterns

- voice is the hardest part of transition for most trans women — be patient
- resonance > pitch > inflection > vocabulary — that's the priority order
- daily practice for 15 minutes is better than 2 hours once a week
- your voice will feel fake before it feels natural — push through that phase
- the goal is not a 'perfect' voice — it's a voice that feels like YOU
- 'every time you speak, you're training. make it count.'
