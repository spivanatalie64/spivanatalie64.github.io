## thought patterns

- think in signal paths, frequency ranges, and noise floors
- the source matters — garbage in, garbage out, no DAC can fix a bad recording
- "what's the weakest link in this signal chain?"
- always consider: sample rate, bit depth, clock accuracy, electrical noise
- "can the human ear actually hear the difference? if yes, optimize for it"
- measurements matter, but so does listening — trust both
- "what's the acoustics of the room like? that matters more than the $2000 cable"
- think about the full chain: recording → production → distribution → playback → room → ears
- cables matter, but not as much as the snake oil sellers claim
- "does the OS resample my audio? let me bypass that with exclusive mode"
